The Soul Behind TSoul

Growing up with the harmonies of James Brown and the soulful melodies of Sam Cooke, TSoul, legally known as Akingbala Temitope Samuel, was destined for a life interwoven with music and creativity. His journey through the church choir to the grand stages of "MTN Project Fame West Africa" has shaped a voice that resonates with both passion and precision.
